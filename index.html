BACKGROUND

-PROGRESS

I'm glad that you're here. I'm really excited to share our story with you.

The "C's" in C's Delight stand for my mother's name, Cheryl, and my girlfriend's name, Christina.

We're both College students pursuing our bachelor's degrees in computer science first year college. One day, we realized why not we start a business at the age of 18. So, since we're dedicated and passionate about what we want, we saved money from our allowances every day to have some income to start a small business.

We believe that "taking a risk is better than regret." When we had the right amount of money to buy equipment (machines) and ingredients, we tried our best to perfect a mini donut mixture.

after many tries We started selling it to friends and family. I told them to give us feedback to make it even better, and they did. So, we used their advice to improve our mini-donut mixture, until we perfect our mixture So We decided to sell our donuts near San Isidro, Rizal, and we're glad that people not only like it but also appreciate it.

Our journey had its highs and lows, but it proved that determination and our mindset can lead to great things. We're excited to see where this adventure takes us next.





Seeing our customers smile while enjoying our mini donuts warmed our hearts. This motivated us to keep trying new flavors and ideas, even when we faced challenges.


We sincerely want to thank everyone. We love you!
